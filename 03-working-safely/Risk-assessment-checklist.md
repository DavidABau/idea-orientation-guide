# Risk Assessment Checklist

Use this checklist when you want a **quick, practical safety read** on whether continuing to engage with an idea is likely to be:

* grounding and helpful
* neutral but intense
* or psychologically risky right now

This is not about judging the *truth* of an idea.
It is about assessing **your current capacity to engage with it safely**.
